About Fortnite

Fortnite debuted in 2017 as Fortnite Save the World, a PVE game in which players work together to build forts and save the world from zombies. The game have trouble reaching a large following despite a generous backing campaign so the Fortnite team threw together a recently-at-the-time Battle Royale mode to drum up interest for their main offering. However, Fortnite Battle Roayale skyrocketed to Popularity and soon became the main focus for the team and is still one of the most popular games today. On this site you can expect to find latestest Fortnite news as well as info on the game's popularity throughout the years, the coolest skins, and a vbuck converter so you will know how much money you need to buy that skin you've had your eye on.
